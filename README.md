# Customer Churn Prediction

## Business Question
Which customers are most likely to cancel, and what drives it?

## Finding
Customers who churn pay $74/month on average vs $61 for loyal customers.
Monthly charges are the single biggest predictor of churn.

## Model
Random Forest Classifier — 75% accuracy on holdout test data.

## Data
7,043 telecom customers. 26% churn rate.

## Tools
Python, Pandas, Scikit-learn, Matplotlib, Google Colab

## Recommendation
Flag customers on high monthly plans with no long-term contract 
for proactive retention outreach before they cancel.
